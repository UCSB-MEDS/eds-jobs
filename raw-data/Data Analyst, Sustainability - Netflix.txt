Data Analyst, Sustainability - Netflix
* Los Gatos, California, Los Angeles, California
* Finance
Responsibilities
* This role will sit with the Netflix Sustainability Office within Finance to support our data-driven sustainability  strategy. Specifically, the role will:
* Collaborate with Production Finance, Product, and other stakeholders (e.g., Data Science Engineering) to source, automate, and analyze data from company operations, including production, to measure and monitor greenhouse gas emissions and other sustainability-related metrics 
* Utilize internal software applications to source, cleanse and analyze a variety of greenhouse gas and spend data, with a special focus on historical production spend data 
* Research, assess, and implement software solutions to automate, visualize and analyze data across Netflix 
* Perform scenario and ad-hoc analyses to inform sustainability strategies and opportunities for the most meaningful reductions
* Update business information tools, spreadsheets, and presentations to ensure they reflect the latest information, and identify improvements to our workflow
* Review and ensure data is complete and meets expectations
* Understand and communicate current production, studio, and corporate workflows, to identify opportunities to improve and automate the data collection process
* Help engage corporate, studio and production personnel, as needed
Qualifications
* Candidates are required to be highly quantitative and analytical, with the ability to communicate clearly and accurately 
* Ideal candidates will have experience in corporate-level greenhouse gas accounting, monitoring and measurement, production finance, and/or studio finance & operations and/or greenhouse gas accounting
* The successful candidate must display initiative in anticipating and exceeding the business demands of senior-level business managers. This person must thrive in a fast-paced, ambiguous environment and exhibit the highest personal and professional standards of integrity and ethics
* Candidates must be able to analyze large datasets and communicate any gaps, questions, and/or assumptions in a concise and clear manner to accomplish their intent
* Netflix is fast-growing with a flat organizational structure, which means that employees are often asked to perform tasks with a range of difficulties. Candidate must be motivated, disciplined, and able to work effectively autonomously and understand when to ask necessary questions
Requirements
* 5+ years of experience in GHG accounting, corporate finance, studio finance and/or production operations, management consulting, investment banking, or accounting is preferred
* Experience conducting quantitative and qualitative financial and operational analyses and working with complex and unstructured datasets
* Comfort working in multiple software packages and recommending how workflows can be streamlined where needed
* Excellent written and oral communication skills
* An appetite to drive impact individually and as a team member
* Bachelor’s degree with emphasis on coursework in Business, Finance, or Accounting, climate change, greenhouse gas accounting,  and/or Environmental Science
