Research Interdisciplinary Biologist/Computer Scientist GS-0401/1550
Salary: $77,488.00 (Step 01) to (Step 10); $100,739.00
Duties
As an Interdisciplinary Data Scientist within the National Wildlife Health Center, some of your specific duties will include:
* Develop and implement novel data science approaches to better understand and predict wildlife diseases.
* Oversee the design and creation of analysis and processing workflows, including detailed, well documented, and automated program code, for structured and unstructured data.
* Generates collaborative research projects that develop new data science techniques, and applies appropriate methods towards prediction, reproducibility, and communication.
* Communicate research findings to both technical and non-technical audiences, and results are used to inform management decisions.
* Develop data visualization tools to aid interpretation and use of complex modeling results by natural resource managers.
