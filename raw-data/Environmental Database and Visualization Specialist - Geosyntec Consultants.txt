Essential Duties And Responsibilites


Is this the right position to help you achieve your professional goals? Some of the key responsibilities for the position include:
* Designing, developing, and managing project-specific relational database applications using desktop (MS Access) and enterprise (MS SQL Server, EQuIS) database software;
* Developing comprehensive workflows and metadata to automate compilation of data from multiple sources and serve data to maps, reports and other tools;
* Compiling, verifying, analyzing, and reporting large and diverse datasets;
* Improving workflows and process efficiency using programming languages (e.g. R, Python, VBA);
* Interacting with internal and external providers to coordinate collection and transfer of data to project databases; and
* Interacting with internal and external clients to support database management and data visualization for environmental engineering, geoscience, stormwater, and sustainable groundwater management projects.
Collaboration and mentoring are cornerstones of Geosyntec’s culture. You should expect to work closely with experienced professionals who will guide and integrate you into Geosyntec’s culture, as you kick start your career. Your success is our success. We encourage our professionals to continually develop their interests and skills. Advancement is based on an individual’s own performance and initiative.


EDUCATION AND LICENSURE
* BS degree in environmental science, geography, computer science, or geology. (required)
* Master’s degree in environmental science, geography, computer science, or geology. (preferred)
Skills, Experience And Qualifications
* At least 1 year of technical training and experience in database technology or GIS; or equivalent combination of education, experience and training that provides the prerequisite knowledge and skills. (preferred)
* Exceptional attention to detail in quantitative work products, written communication, spelling, and grammar. (required)
* Proficiency in MS Excel and MS Access, including the ability to write complex formulas and visualize data effectively. (required)
* Working knowledge of database structure, query design, and maintenance. (required)
* Proficiency in scripting languages such as R, Python, SQL, or Matlab. (preferred)
* Proficiency in web-based mapping applications. (preferred)
* Competency in, or ability to learn three-dimensional (3D) modeling and visualization software (e.g. Earth Volumetric Studio (EVS), Leapfrog3D, etc.). (preferred)
* Knowledge of server architecture and web technology. (preferred)
* Experience with the front-end processes of collecting field data with various GPS units and the process of downloading and compiling the data. (preferred)
* Understanding and use of Graphic Design software (Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.). (preferred)
* Direct experience managing environmental, geologic, and geotechnical data collection. (preferred)
* Knowledge of laboratory analytical data verification and validation processes. (preferred)
* Valid U.S. driver’s license and satisfactory driving record for business travel. (required)
Preferred Candidates Will Also Possess The Following Professional Attributes
* Vision and desire to lead a data management and visualization practice in the environmental consulting industry.
* Patience and planning skills to cultivate an efficient data management and visualization practice.
* Ability to talk to and engage clients in business development and willingness to learn new business development skills.
